The Way Out

Trapped. Cursed. Transformed.

Once a brave knight.
Now a tiny bug.
A mischievous wizard has trapped you inside a matchbox labyrinth, stripping you of your armor and leaving only your wits.
There is no sword.
No magic spell.
Only the path you create.

A STORY OF TRANSFORMATION

From Knight to Bug

So basically, The Way Out mixes storytelling with puzzle-solving in a pretty cool way.
You are not just solving a maze — you are a fallen knight navigating a magical prison.
The wizard’s curse has shrunk you, forcing you to think differently. Strength no longer matters. Only logic and foresight remain.
Every level represents a new chamber within the enchanted matchbox.
You can't just stumble your way out - you've gotta actually build your escape route piece by piece.

HOW TO PLAY

Build the Path to Freedom

The mechanics are easy to grasp:

  • Drag and drop labyrinth pieces onto the grid

  • Connect paths carefully

  • Avoid dead ends

  • Guide your character toward the exit

But planning ahead is essential.
Place tiles too quickly, and you may trap yourself.
Think carefully, and the route will reveal itself.
Sometimes you'll just have a gut feeling about where to place a tile, but you also need to think a few moves ahead.
